# Background

My path into tech started around 2019 in secondary school, writing C++ for the first time. Not pretty, but it was a start.

I spent a few years in a bachelor IT program. The scope was massive — data structures, UI/UX, mobile apps, game development all at once. Hard to stay motivated when nothing feels connected and you're the kind of person who needs to actually care about what they're building.

The graduaat at AP Hogeschool clicked better. Every subject built on the last. And somewhere along the way I started building things outside of school just because I wanted to. That's usually when I learn the most.
